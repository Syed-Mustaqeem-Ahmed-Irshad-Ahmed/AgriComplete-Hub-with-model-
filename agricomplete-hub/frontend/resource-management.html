Pesticide & Safety Guide

Weather Condition

Avoid spraying if temperatures are above 30°C or humidity is below 40% as it can cause crop burn.

Personal Safety

Always wear protective gear (mask, gloves, boots) and spray in the direction of the wind, never against it.

Dosage Control

Follow the recommended dosage on the label. High concentrations do NOT mean better results.
